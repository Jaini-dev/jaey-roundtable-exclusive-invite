The Conversation

We Are Ready to Go There

Nine threads. Nothing off-limits.

01
"Beta sambhalega" then why is it always Beti?
Fathers stopped waiting for sons. Nobody announced it. But every daughter in this room felt it.
02
The Investor's Squint
She walked in with a pitch deck and five years of proof. He leaned back and asked who her co-founder was.
03
Two Homes, Zero Time for Yourself
Her parents miss her. His parents need her. She is the emotional glue of two homes and still shows up for herself last.
04
He's "Assertive." She's "Difficult."
Same boardroom. Same tone. Same words. He gets a promotion. She gets a reputation.
05
My Body Has a Diagnosis. My Calendar Doesn't Care.
PCOD. PCOS. Endometriosis. Chronic, invisible, and somehow expected to stay private. She shows up anyway. Always.
06
We Chose Not to Have Kids. Now What?
A decision they made together. But only one of them is asked to justify it at every family dinner.
07
The Loneliness at the Top
She made it. She is in the room. And she is completely, quietly alone up there.
08
The Guilt That Comes With Every Win
She closed the deal, got the promotion, launched the brand. And then the guilt. About not being home. About wanting more.
09
Mental Health Is Not a Weakness. Saying So Out Loud Is.
High-achieving women are the last to admit burnout. Because the world reads her vulnerability as incompetence.
